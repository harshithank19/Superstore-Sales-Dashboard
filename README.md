## 📌 Overview

The dashboard analyzes a Superstore's sales transactions across regions, categories, and time periods — providing a clear picture of sales performance, profitability trends, and customer behavior.

**Key KPIs tracked:**
- Total Sales: **1.57M**
- Total Profit: **175.26K**
- Total Quantity Sold: **22K**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

| Visual | Insight |
|--------|---------|
| Cards | Total Sales, Profit, Quantity at a glance |
| Pie Charts | Sales by Payment Mode & Customer Segment |
| Bar Charts | Category-wise and Sub-category-wise sales |
| Line Graphs | Monthly & yearly profit and sales trends |
| Maps | Regional sales distribution across U.S. states |
| Forecasting | Sales forecasting by order date |

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Office Supplies** contributes the highest sales among all categories
- **Standard Class** shipping is the most preferred mode
- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** is the most used payment method
- Seasonal sales spikes observed in **November and December**
- **West region** leads in overall sales performance

## 🚀 How to Use

3. Open `BI project.pbix` in Power BI Desktop
4. Explore the interactive dashboard with region and year filters
